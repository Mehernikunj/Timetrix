Welcome to the Timetable Generator

Our timetable generator is a modern, feature-rich tool designed for schools, colleges, and individuals to plan their schedules effectively. The purpose of this project is to simplify task and subject management, ensuring that time is utilized efficiently. It allows users to input their daily tasks, set durations, and generate a complete timetable automatically.

With our timetable generator, you can: - Create multiple tasks or subjects in one session. - Input start times and durations for each task. - Automatically calculate start and end times for all entries. - Generate visually appealing, printable schedules. - Save your timetables locally for easy retrieval.

This project aims to reduce manual effort in planning, increase productivity, and provide a seamless scheduling experience for students, teachers, and professionals alike. By integrating real-time saving capabilities and an admin panel for customization, it caters to both personal and institutional needs. Whether you are preparing for exams, planning lectures, or organizing a personal day-to-day schedule, this tool ensures everything runs smoothly.

The project also emphasizes ease of use. It features a clean, intuitive design with light colors to ensure a pleasant user experience. Accessibility and flexibility are prioritized, making the timetable generator suitable for a diverse range of users. Join us in optimizing time management and achieving your goals efficiently with this innovative solution.
